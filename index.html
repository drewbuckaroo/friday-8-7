About me

My name is Drew Lockhart. I'm originally from Idaho and moved to Portland, Oregon in 2013. Epicodus is my first attempt at higher education beyond my high school diploma. I decided to branch out and give Epicodus a try after a friend of mine with a similarly lived lifestyle graduated and spoke highly of the program.
